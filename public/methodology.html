Our Methodology

Our ranking methodology is designed to be objective, transparent, and consistent, using public data to evaluate university performance across key areas.

Detailed Evaluation Metrics

Research (40%)

Assesses the scope, quality, and influence of a university's scholarly output across disciplines.

Reputation (25%)

Reflects how the institution is regarded by the global academic and professional communities.

Employment (25%)

Gauges the career success and workplace impact of graduates in diverse industries.

Internationalization (10%)

Measures the institution's global reach and diversity in students, faculty, and collaborations.

Data Sources & Collection

We rely on publicly available and third-party data to ensure objectivity and verifiability.
