Import Instructions

The uploaded file must be in CSV or XLSX format. The column headers are case-insensitive.

Required Fields

  • University - University Name
  • Country - Country
  • Total_Score - Total Score

Optional Fields

  • Rank - Ranking
  • Research - Research Score
  • Reputation - Reputation Score
  • Employment - Employment Score
  • International - Internationalization Score
  • Star_Rating - Star Rating
  • year - Year

Clear Data

This action will permanently delete all university data from the database. This cannot be undone.
